Classes

The Tipsters' classes vary from Science to advanced Coding classes. Once they select their top 3 choices for the type of class they want to participate in, they choose the campus in which they want to learn their chosen subject. Students don't always get in the class that they want, but it is almost guaraneeted to learn something new and have fun. By the end of the term, the Tipsters will go home with advanced education on a certain subject that will broaden the spectrum of jobs in the future.

Social Functions

Every weekday, 6 different evening activities are offered. Students select their top 3 choices, the day before. The activities range from athletic-based events to simply playing card games.
